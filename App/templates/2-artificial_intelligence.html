Artificial Intelligence

The Importance of Creating a Culture of Data


The linked blog post speaks to the importance of creating a culture of data.

Firstly, artificial intelligence, or AI for short, is a wide ranging branch of computer science which is concerned with building smart machines capable of performing tasks which would typically require human intelligence.

It is to be noted that artificial intelligence requires machine learning and machine learning requires analytics. To work with analytics effectively, elegant data or information architecture (IA) is needed.

When using artificial intelligence, the real success comes down to the organization's ability to adopt a culture which is focused or centered on data as a culture can be one an extremely powerful asset or an obstruction for a company or organization.


"Every organization that aspires to a data culture has to pick a place to start. Deep learning will make data accessible that previously was not; if that will create momentum, with a high chance of success, that is where you should start. For other organizations, better predictions and automation will beget a data culture. Regardless of which path you choose, the objective is the same: do data science faster."
